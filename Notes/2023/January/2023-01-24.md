### Symmetric group

$S_n$ \
Permutations on a set on $n$ objects form a group

### Composition

Let $\pi, \sigma \in S_9$ \
$\pi = (1\ 3\ 5)(4\ 6)(2\ 7\ 8\ 9)$ \
$\sigma = (1\ 4\ 7\ 9)(2\ 3)(5)(6\ 8)$ \
Calculate ${\pi}\circ{\sigma}$ \
${\pi}\circ{\sigma} = (1\ 2\ 3\ 4\ 5)(6\ 7)$
