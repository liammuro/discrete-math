### MODULAR ARITHMETIC

$a \equiv b \pmod{n} \\ a,b \in \Z, n > 1$

1. $a$ and $b$ have same remainder when divided by $n$

2. $C$ is a set. For a given k: \
$C = \set{x \in \Z : x \pmod{n} = k} \\ a,b \in C \\ k \in \Z$

3. $a = k \times n + b, \\ k \in \Z$

4. $n \mid (a-b)$ \
$(a-b)$ is a multiple of $n$
