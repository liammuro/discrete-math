### GCD

$ \begin{aligned}
    \gcd(1820, 231) \\
    1820 &= 231 (7) + 203 \\
    231 &= 203 (1) + 28 \\
    203 &= 28(7) + 7 \\
    28 &= 7(4) + 0 \\
    \gcd(1820, 231) &= 7
\end{aligned}$

### Congruence Modulo

Congruencies are equivalence classes

$ \begin{aligned}
\gcd(1739, 29341) \\
29341 &= 1739(16) + 1517 \\
1739 &= 1517(1) + 222 \\
1517 &= 222(6) + 185 \\
222 &= 185(1) + 37 \\
185 &= 37(5) + 0 \\
\gcd(1739, 29341) &= 37
\end{aligned}$
